Intake System

Caregiver Applications — Pain Point Solved

✅

No more caregivers reaching out through personal channels

Every caregiver who wants to work with Clara's CareTeam now applies through the website at /caregiving-opportunities. Their information goes straight into the system — organized, tracked, and instantly notified to the right people.

Here is exactly what happens the moment a caregiver submits their application:

  • Their full application is saved to the secure database instantly
  • A Telegram notification fires to the claracareteamTX group immediately (see below)
  • The caregiver receives a professional confirmation email from info@claracareteam.com
  • Their application appears in the Admin dashboard under Staff → Applicants
  • The team can move them through the pipeline: Applicant → Hired → Active
📱 Telegram Group — claracareteamTX

All three members of the core team are in the claracareteamTX group. This is your real-time command center. Every significant event in the system fires a notification straight to this group — no email inbox to monitor, no dashboard to keep checking. It comes directly to you.

The Operating System

ClaraCare OS — Every Module, Live and Ready

Behind the public website lives a complete care operations platform built exclusively for Clara's CareTeam. It runs at claracareteam.com/admin and is PIN-protected — only authorised team members can access it. Every module below is live and ready to use today.

📊

Dashboard Overview

9-stat command view — active clients, scheduled shifts, open applications, pending invoices, medication adherence, and more. Time-aware personalised greeting on login.

👥

Staff Management

Full caregiver lifecycle — Applicant → Interviewed → Hired → Active → Inactive. Profile pages with credentials, role, status, and assignment history.

🏠

Client CRM

Complete client database. Each client has a profile, care history, linked care recipients, and all associated shifts and assignments in one place.

🧓

Care Recipients

Individual patient profiles — separate from the billing client. Track each person receiving care, their needs, care plan, and assigned staff.

📅

Scheduling & Rota

Weekly shift calendar with caregiver assignments. Conflict detection prevents double-booking. Hours engine tracks time worked per staff member.

💊

Medication Tracking (MAR)

Medication Administration Records. Log what was given, when, and by whom. Adherence overview flags missed medications. Full audit trail per patient.

📋

Care Plans

Create and manage individual care plans per recipient. Mobile-optimised editor — switch between patient list and plan editor with a single tap on your phone.

🧾

Billing & Invoices

Auto-generate invoices from completed shifts. Track paid/unpaid status. Telegram notifies the team when an invoice is marked paid. Full line-item breakdown.

📄

Reports

Generate MAR reports, shift summaries, task completion, and compliance reports. Set a date range, select a recipient, export a clean professional report.

✅

Compliance Hub

Stay survey-ready. Track documentation status, flag gaps, and keep a complete compliance record — built specifically for home care agency standards.

⚡

Operations Center

Daily command center — see today's shifts, task completion, medication progress, and active alerts at a glance. Built for the start of every shift day.

📧

Email Inbox

The info@claracareteam.com inbox is built directly into the admin panel. Read and manage emails without ever leaving the dashboard.

🤖

AI Tools Hub

6 AI-powered tools: Care Plan Generator, Patient Summary, Shift Match AI, Report Enhancer, Incident Analyzer, and Intake Data Extractor.

🌐

Website CMS

Control public website content from within the admin panel — edit testimonials, FAQs, and site settings without touching any code.

📱

Telegram Notifications

Real-time alerts to claracareteamTX for new caregiver applications, care requests, contact form submissions, and invoice paid events.

📲

Full Mobile Support

Every admin page is optimised for phone use — iOS safe areas, mobile panel toggles, bottom navigation, full-screen modals. Manage everything from your phone.
